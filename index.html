About

Santa's Inc is all about getting fast, timed deliveries of toys for nice kids that did behave during the year (and not just a few weeks before christmas - those are just opportunists!).

We are making nice, old fashioned toys during all the year, collecting orders starting 1st of December, and make all our deliveries, all around the world in the night between the 24th and 25th of December. No deliveries are possible before that date.
